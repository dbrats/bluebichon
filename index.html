Games

Ashen Daughter

Ashen Daughter is our upcoming debut title: a hand-drawn 2D Metroidvania adventure set in a mythic world inspired by Norse legends.

Players take on the role of Kára, a determined young woman brought back to life by the Last Whisper, a mysterious divine being, as she seeks justice against those who destroyed her village. The game features fast, responsive movement, intense combat, exploration, and hidden upgrades across a beautifully crafted, story-rich landscape.

More updates will follow soon!
